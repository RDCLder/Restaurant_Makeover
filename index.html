WELCOME TO DADONG

Taste the best of China in the heart of New York City.

China's most famous and influential chef, DaDong, has opened his first U.S. restaurant just east of Times Square at 3 Bryant Park. Taste his iconic Roast Peking Duck in addition to his full menu of refined Chinese cuisine inside the stunning two-floor space.

Join in on the sexy vibrance of the energetic second floor dining room complete with a large outdoor terrace with full seating. Or, sit within the refined elegance of the third floor with an additional smaller outdoor space. For a private dinner or event, book one of the exquisite private dining rooms.
